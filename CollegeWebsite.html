About Us

Krishna Institute of Technology (KIOT) Kanpur, established in 2008, is a private technical institution approved by AICTE and affiliated with AKTU, Lucknow. Located in Mandhana, it offers UG/PG programs in Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, and Nursing with a focus on practical skill development and a reported 75-80% placement rate in companies like TCS and Infosys.
